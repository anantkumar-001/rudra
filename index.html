About Rudra Agro Mills

Who We Are at GIRI

A Bhiwadi-based traditional agri-processing unit you can trust.

We are dedicated to producing premium quality staples trusted by families everywhere. Every product that leaves our mill undergoes rigorous quality checks to ensure you get nothing but the best.

Learn More
Rooted in Tradition

Started small. Grew with integrity and hard work.

Started as a small traditional mill, we have steadily grown our operations and reach to serve pan-India while maintaining our strict quality standards. Our journey is paved with hard work, a commitment to natural processes, and an unwavering focus on consumer health.

Learn More
A Healthier India

Making pure food accessible to every Indian household.

Our vision is to make pure, unadulterated food accessible to every household, enriching lives with the natural goodness of the earth. We believe that healthy food should not be a luxury, but a staple right for every family across the nation.

Learn More
Quality at Every Step

Zero adulteration. Farm-to-table at every step.

To implement stringent quality checks, ensure zero adulteration, and maintain a seamless farm-to-table process for every product we deliver. We work closely with farmers to source the best raw materials, processing them with care to preserve their nutritional integrity.
